[ tīy ]  
  
தீ, II. வி. i. be slightly scorched or burnt (as food) கருகு;  
2. be scorched by the heat of the sun, காய்  
தீ, VI. வி. t. singe, scorch slightly in cooking;  
2. dry up water (as the sun or fire) காயச்செய்
