[ pira ]  
  
part. pra. A Sanskrit prefix; ஒருவடமொழியுபசர்க்கம்  
pref. expressing forth, forward (as in பிரயோகம்);  
2. very much (as in பிரகோபம்);  
3. appearing, becoming public (as in பிரசித்தம்). Compounds பார். in their places
