below  
  
[ kīẕē ]  
கீழ் kiiRe கீழெ  
1. a place below, down; bottom; the east (cf. கிழக்கு)  
2. below, under (touching*) [post. + obl.]  
3. below, under (not touching*) [post. + dat.]  
*contrast not kept, in all dialects
