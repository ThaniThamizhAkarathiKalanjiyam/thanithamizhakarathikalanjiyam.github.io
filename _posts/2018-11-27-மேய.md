adj. (poetical for மேவ), suitable, apt, appropriate, தகுந்த  
[ mēy ]  
[poet. form of மேவ.]  
Suitable. மேயநல்லிருக்கை. An appropriate seat, a seat suited to ones rank. (காசிநாண்.)
